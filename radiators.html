Radiator Installation
Dysons Heating Ltd designs, supplies, and installs heating systems for all property types, from small flats to large estates or listed buildings. Specializing in wet radiator and underfloor heating systems, our team has the expertise to provide the perfect heating solution tailored to your property's needs.
The Benefits
We offer magnetic system flushing to eliminate cold spots on radiators, improving heat distribution, energy efficiency, and potentially lowering energy bills. Additionally, we provide landlord gas safety inspections and gas safety reports, ensuring your heating system runs safely and efficiently, meeting all necessary safety regulations and performance standards.
